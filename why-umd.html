Why I Chose the University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because it offers the perfect combination of academic quality, personal connection, and natural beauty. I was especially drawn to UMD's strong business school, which provides practical, hands-on learning experiences that align with my career goals. The smaller class sizes allow for more meaningful interactions with professors and classmates, creating a more engaging learning environment. I also value UMD's inclusive and welcoming community, where students are encouraged to be involved and supported in their growth. In addition, the location near Lake Superior adds to the campus's appeal, offering a unique and inspiring setting for both studying and everyday life.

What I Like About Duluth and the Surrounding Area

Duluth is a city that combines opportunity and beauty in a way few other places can. The surrounding area offers countless outdoor activities, from hiking and skiing to simply enjoying the lakewalk or the views along the North Shore. The proximity to Lake Superior gives the city a calm, refreshing atmosphere that makes it a great place to live and learn. At the same time, Duluth's downtown area provides plenty of restaurants, shops, and entertainment options, creating a balance between nature and city life that I really appreciate.

Natural Beauty

The stunning Lake Superior shoreline, numerous hiking trails, and scenic overlooks make Duluth a perfect location for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.
